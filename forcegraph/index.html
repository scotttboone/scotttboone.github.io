Metageography

Clustering of geographic regions based on common attributes. The link between each geographic region receives 1 "point" of strength for each parameter in which the two regions are within one standard deviation of each other; likewise, one "point" is subtracted for each parameter in which the regions differ by more than two standard deviations.

A force-directed simulation then clusters the regions into groups sharing an abundance of similar (and a lack of divergent) characteristics.
